# 🚀 Backend & AI-Architect Roadmap: 2026

A high-intensity, 14-week "Stealth-Mode" execution plan to master scalable system design and AI integration.

---

## 🏗️ Phase 1: Distributed Systems & Backend Mastery

**Focus:** Infrastructure, Performance, and Scaling Logic.
**Timeline:** 4–6 Weeks

- **Advanced System Design:** Deep dive into high-availability architecture, CAP theorem, and data consistency.
- **Performance Engineering:** Real-world implementation of Caching (Redis) and Message Queues (RabbitMQ/Kafka).
- **Database Scalability:** SQL vs. NoSQL optimization, indexing strategies, and sharding.
- **Real-time Systems:** Mastering WebSockets and low-latency communication.

---

## 🤖 Phase 2: AI Engineering (RAG & LLM Ops)

**Focus:** Building context-aware AI systems.
**Timeline:** 3–4 Weeks

- **Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG):** Implementing advanced retrieval, chunking strategies, and re-ranking.
- **Vector Orchestration:** Hands-on with Vector Databases (Pinecone, Milvus, or pgvector).
- **Embeddings & Context:** Developing efficient pipelines for data ingestion and semantic search.
- **LLM Integration:** Fine-tuning AI prompts for system design assistance and code evaluation.

---

## 🧩 Phase 3: Architecture-Driven Integration

**Focus:** The "Golden Phase" – Embedding AI into core system architecture.
**Timeline:** 4–6 Weeks

- **Intelligent Services:** Building systems where AI lives _inside_ the backend (e.g., AI-driven notification routing or load balancing).
- **Scalable SaaS Modules:** Developing AI-powered extensions for the **Classmatic** ecosystem.
- **Full-Stack Synthesis:** Bridging the gap between a robust Go/Node.js backend and reactive AI-driven frontends.
- **Testing & Monitoring:** Evaluating AI accuracy and system performance under high load.

---

| Deadline    | Milestone                                                                   |
